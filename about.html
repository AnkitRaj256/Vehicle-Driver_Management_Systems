About Us

Welcome to our Vehicle-Driver Management System, designed to streamline the process of assigning vehicles to drivers. Our platform offers an intuitive interface for creating and managing driver profiles, assigning vehicles, scheduling assignments, and handling potential conflicts.

Key Features:

  • Driver Management: Easily create and manage driver profiles with essential details like Driver ID, Name, Email, and Phone.
  • Vehicle Assignment: Assign vehicles to drivers with simple dropdown menus, ensuring each driver is assigned to one vehicle at a time.
  • Time Scheduling: Schedule assignments with start and end times, avoiding double bookings.
  • Conflict Handling: Advanced logic prevents overlapping assignments, maintaining smooth operations.
  • Location-Based Search: Find and assign drivers based on location and availability for optimal efficiency.

Our commitment is to provide a user-friendly, reliable platform that meets your fleet management needs. Thank you for choosing us to support your operations.
